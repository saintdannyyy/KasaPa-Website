About KasaPa

Our Story

KasaPa was born during the Tɛkyerɛma Pa Hackathon 2025, where Team Twilight envisioned a solution for bridging communication gaps for speech-impaired individuals in Ghana. Against 48 competing teams, we secured 3rd place with our innovative approach to inclusive technology.

"We built KasaPa to help bridge the communication gap for people with speech impairments in Ghana."

- Team Twilight

Our Mission

Inclusive Tech for Social Good - We leverage AI to address real-world challenges, particularly in local communities with limited access to advanced tech solutions.

3rd Place out of 48 teams
2025 Tɛkyerɛma Pa Hackathon

Features

Speech-to-Text (STT)

Converts Akan Disorded Speech into text.
Enables speech-impaired individuals to be understood in local contexts using the Twi dialect.

  • Optimized for Ghanaian accents
  • Close to Real-time transcription
  • Local language support

Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Converts typed text into speech using a natural Ghanaian accent. Voice synthesis designed to mirror local speech patterns and tonalities.

  • Natural Ghanaian accent
  • Authentic speech patterns
  • Clear pronunciation

Multilingual Roadmap

Future support for multiple Ghanaian languages like Ewe, Dagbani, and more. Vision for expansion into other African languages, promoting linguistic diversity.

  • Ewe language support
  • Dagbani integration
  • Pan-African expansion

Offline Capability Coming Soon

Planned feature: Will be optimized for low-connectivity areas where internet access is limited. Future versions will ensure real-time communication even in remote regions.

  • Will work without internet
  • Rural area support planned
  • Real-time processing capability
